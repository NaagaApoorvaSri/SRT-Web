SUBSYSTEMS

Powertrain

The powertrain is the heart of a vehicle, transforming raw energy from the engine or electric motor into motion that propels the wheels. It's a symphony of engineering brilliance, where components like the transmission, driveshaft, and axles harmonize to deliver power seamlessly.

Chassis

The chassis is the backbone of a vehicle, the sturdy framework that supports every system and component while shaping the ride's character. More than just a foundation, it’s where strength meets agility, balancing safety, stability, and performance. As vehicles evolve, the chassis is becoming lighter, smarter, and more adaptive, laying the groundwork for the next generation of mobility.

Brakes

Brakes are the unsung heroes of every journey, turning raw speed into controlled power. They embody the art of precision, converting kinetic energy into heat to ensure safety and command over the road. From traditional hydraulic systems to cutting-edge regenerative braking in electric vehicles, brakes are evolving to not only stop but also play a key role in energy efficiency and performance.

Suspension

The suspension system is the silent sculptor of every ride, blending comfort with control. It absorbs the imperfections of the road, keeping the tires grounded and the journey smooth. Beyond just dampening bumps, modern suspension systems adapt dynamically, enhancing performance, stability, and safety, making every drive a seamless dance between the vehicle and the terrain.
